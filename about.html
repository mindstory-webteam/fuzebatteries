About Fuze Batteries

Powering India with
Reliable Energy
 Solutions.

Fuze is a leading lead-acid battery manufacturing company offering high-performance inverter batteries across India for both domestic and commercial use. Since its establishment in 2004, Fuze has been energizing thousands of homes and offices nationwide with dependable power solutions.

Built on innovation and dedication, Fuze specializes in revolutionary Tubular Batteries and Solar Batteries designed to meet modern energy demands. Our continuous commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us overcome every challenge and grow into a trusted battery manufacturer in India.

OUR VISION

To serve customers across India with innovative, reliable, and future-ready power solutions that support homes, businesses, and the nation’s growing energy needs.

OUR MISSION

To lead the battery industry by developing dependable, high-performance products while building long-term trust and positioning ourselves as one of the most reliable battery manufacturers in India.

OUR CORE VALUES

We foster a friendly, humble, and collaborative work culture rooted in teamwork and integrity. Through continuous innovation, ethical practices, and a commitment to premium quality, we strive to serve our customers and our country with excellence and earn unparalleled trust.
