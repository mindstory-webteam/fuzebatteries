About Fuze Batteries

Powering India with
Reliable Energy
 Solutions.

Fuze is a leading lead-acid battery manufacturing company offering high-performance inverter batteries across India for both domestic and commercial use. Since its establishment in 2012, Fuze has been energizing thousands of homes and offices nationwide with dependable power solutions.

Built on innovation and dedication, Fuze specializes in revolutionary Tubular Batteries and Solar Batteries designed to meet modern energy demands. Our continuous commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us overcome every challenge and grow into a trusted battery manufacturer in India.
