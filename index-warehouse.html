FAQ

Everything You Should Know About Our Services

  • Storage & Handling
  • Access & Support
  • Billing & Documentation
  • What warehouse services do you provide?
    We provide storage, inventory management, order fulfillment, cross-docking, and distribution services for businesses of all sizes.
    How do I start using your warehouse?
    Simply contact us or request a storage plan online. Our team will guide you through setup, terms, and available options.
    Do you handle temperature-sensitive goods?
    Yes, we provide cold storage and climate-controlled facilities for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and sensitive materials.
    What is included in warehouse services?
    Our services cover receiving, storage, order processing, packaging, dispatching, and real-time inventory reporting.
    How long can I store my goods?
    Storage duration is flexible based on your plan. Short-term and long-term warehousing options are available to fit your needs.
  • How can I access my inventory?
    You can monitor inventory anytime using our online warehouse management system with your client ID and login details.
    Do you provide real-time inventory updates?
    Yes, we provide real-time stock updates via dashboard, email, or mobile app so you know your inventory status at all times.
    What if I need urgent access?
    In urgent cases, our warehouse team will arrange priority access and coordinate retrieval or dispatch of your goods quickly.
    Can I change my storage plan later?
    Yes, storage plans can be adjusted based on your inventory needs. Additional space and services are available anytime.
    Do you offer customer support 24/7?
    Yes, our warehouse support team is available 24/7 to assist with inquiries, inventory updates, and urgent requests.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept bank transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and corporate billing accounts for approved business clients.
    Do you provide storage invoices?
    Yes, every service includes a detailed invoice with storage fees, handling charges, and any additional services used.
    How are storage fees calculated?
    Fees are based on space, duration, and service level. We help calculate accurate costs with clear documentation provided.
    Can I request a cost breakdown?
    Absolutely. We provide a transparent cost breakdown for warehousing, handling, and distribution charges.
    Do you offer credit terms for businesses?
    Yes, flexible billing and credit terms are available for qualified business accounts upon approval.
