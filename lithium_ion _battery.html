Why Choose Fuze Lithium Batteries?

Inbuilt Lithium-Ion Battery for Compact Design

Say goodbye to bulky battery setups. Fuze Lithium-Ion UPS features an inbuilt lithium-ion battery with a compact, wall-mounted design that blends seamlessly into modern homes and commercial spaces.

Fast Charging Technology

The Fuze 1100 model charges fully in just 4 hours and 30 minutes using a 25-amp fast charger. Higher-capacity models such as 2200 VA and 5500 VA are also optimized for rapid charging, ensuring power readiness at all times.

Advanced Battery Management System (BMS)

Equipped with an intelligent Battery Management System (BMS), Fuze UPS ensures automatic voltage cut-offs, protecting both the battery and connected appliances while extending overall battery life.

Long Life & High Durability

Designed for long-term performance, Fuze Lithium-Ion UPS supports up to 5,000 charge-discharge cycles and delivers a battery life of up to 10 years—perfect for Kerala’s power conditions.

24x7 Protection with Built-in MCB

Safety comes first with a built-in Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), offering continuous protection against overloads and short circuits for worry-free operation.

Zero Maintenance, Zero Hassles

No acid spills, no water refills, and no toxic fumes. Enjoy clean, silent, and maintenance-free power backup from a trusted Lithium Batteries Manufacturer in Kerala—Fuze.
