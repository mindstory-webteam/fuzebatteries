FAQ

Everything You Should Know About Moving Services

  • Moving & Service
  • Tracking & Support
  • Billing & Documentation
  • What type of moving services do you provide?
    We offer local and long-distance moving, residential and commercial relocation, packing & unpacking, loading & unloading, and secure storage options.
    How do I start booking a move?
    Simply request a quote online or contact our team. We will guide you through inventory assessment, scheduling, pricing options, and recommended service packages.
    Do you handle interstate or international relocation?
    Yes, we handle both domestic long-distance and international relocation with documentation assistance and end-to-end moving coordination.
    What is included in your moving process?
    Our moving process includes professional packing, safe loading, transportation, unloading, placement of items at destination, and optional unpacking service.
    How long does a move usually take?
    Duration depends on distance and volume. Local moves generally take the same day, while long-distance moves typically range from 2–14 days depending on route.
  • How can I track the progress of my move?
    You can track your moving status through our live system using booking number, or contact support for real-time status updates.
    Do you notify when movers are on the way?
    Yes, we provide live ETA updates and driver notification before arrival to ensure proper preparation at pickup and drop-off locations.
    What happens if the move gets delayed?
    If delays occur due to weather or road conditions, our team will notify you immediately and reschedule the nearest available timeline.
    Can I change the moving date after booking?
    Yes, you can request schedule changes prior to dispatch. After dispatch, adjustments may incur additional charges depending on route and availability.
    Do you offer support during the move?
    Yes, our support team is available to assist with timeline questions, coordination, and any urgent requests throughout the entire moving process.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept bank transfer, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and company billing accounts for business clients.
    Do you provide cost estimation and invoice?
    Yes, every moving service includes a detailed breakdown covering packing, transportation, labor, insurance, and extra services if selected.
    How does insurance coverage work?
    Insurance coverage can be added based on total declared value of items. We will help you choose the best coverage type for your belongings.
    Can I request itemized cost details?
    Absolutely. We provide a full transparent breakdown of charges including packing materials, transportation fees, and handling labor.
    Do you offer monthly billing for business accounts?
    Yes, qualified business clients can apply for flexible billing terms and monthly account invoicing.
